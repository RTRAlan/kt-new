Our Company

KT Bolt Manufacturing, Inc. has served the fastener industry since 1969 as a manufacturer of non-standard and custom bolts and fasteners.

A full-service fastener manufacturer and fastener distribution center based in Katy, TX

K-T Bolt Manufacturing is your one-stop-shop for all your fastener manufacturing and distribution needs. Whatever your project requires, our skilled team has you covered. We manufacture custom and non-standard fasteners and operate the highest volume distribution center of nuts and bolts and standard fasteners in the U.S.

We aren’t just in the fastener business, we’re in the people business. We aim to go beyond helping you complete one project. We’re here to build life-long relationships because your projects matter to us because people matter to us.

Hot Forging

K-T Bolt Manufacturing, Inc. hot forges bolts in a variety of head styles and in almost all grades of material. Our heading capacity is from 1/2" diameter through 3” diameter.

K-T Bolt Manufacturing is a leading manufacturer and distributor of fasteners for structural steel fabrication, general and highway construction, mining, railroads, agriculture, and other industries

Houston metro home base

Located just outside of Houston in Katy, Texas, we are well-positioned to meet all your fastener manufacturing and distribution needs.

For over 40 years, we have been a leader in manufacturing and distributing fasteners for multiple and varied industries, specifically structural steel. As of 2022, we are a sister company of Birmingham Fastener, one of the nation’s leading fastener manufacturers and distributors.

Our mission is to provide all of our customers with quality products and superior service in both distribution and manufactured fastener products. We are dedicated to satisfying customer needs on standard products and sourcing hard-to-find items. Through comprehensive inventories and distribution and state-of-the-art specialty manufacturing capabilities, K-T Bolt is widely recognized as an industry leader.

Domestic Fasteners for Critical Construction Industries

K-T Bolt is a leading manufacturer and distributor of bolts for structural steel fabrication, general and highway construction, mining, railroads, agriculture, and other industries. We are proud to have the largest stock of structural bolts nationwide.

Our structural bolts meet all ASTM requirements. We guarantee that all products we manufacture or distribute for the structural steel industry are of the highest quality available.

Along with exceptional products, our team puts a premium on providing exceptional service. We strive to maintain constant contact with all clients. Our team does whatever it takes to make sure our clients in the structural steel industry are beyond satisfied with our products and service.

We also provide additional services for structural steel fabricators including job site assistance, rotational capacity testing at our labs, in-depth packaging services like job packing per sequence, structural bolt assembly, and more.

DEPENDABLE NUTS, BOLTS, FASTENERS, AND MORE

  • A325 and A490 heavy hex bolts (structural)
  • A449 Type 1 and Type 3 in Heavy or Finished Hex
  • A534 Grades BD and BC
  • SAE J429 Grades 5 and 8
  • A193 Grades B7, B6, B8, and B8M

  • Materials: Stainless Steel, 18-8 Stainless, Steel, Bolt Grade, Grade 2, Grade 5, Grade 8, Alloy Steel, Silicon Bronze, Brass, Zinc Plating, Hot Dip Galvanizing, Chrome
