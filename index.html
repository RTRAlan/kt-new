About Us

Our Products

Committed to Service Since 1969

KT Bolt Manufacturing has served the fastener industry since 1969 as a manufacturer of non-standard and custom bolts and fasteners. During this time we have worked hard to develop a strong repuation for providing quality products and excellent service to our valued partners.

Electropolish & Hot-Dip Galvanizing

Our in-house coating facility offers our customers quality coatings for the toughest jobs.

Domestic Bolt Manufacturing

We specialize in hot forging of high temperature and corrosion-resistant stainless fasteners.

KT Bolt is now a member of the Birmingham Fastener Family!

“The future of our company is now and always will be about growth and productive changes. At the same time, we must hold fast to our core values like world-class service, accountability, and a commitment to excellence that we’ve had since my father founded this company in 1980” says Brad Tinney, President and CEO of Birmingham Fastener. “This partnership allows us to further expand our footprint and strengthen our commitment to American fastener manufacturing.”
